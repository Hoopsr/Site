What do we mean by 'Strength Goals'?

Strength Goals will be one of 4 main topics we will be discussing at the ActiveGoals salon. Strength is a key part of both fitness and mental health, but we will be focusing on physical strength here, discussing mental strength within the mental health topic. The aim here is not to compare or judge the individual strength of each other, but to discuss, share experiences and give tips to other participants. Even if you're not one for lifting weights, we would love to have you join in to gain inspiration from others and offer a different perspective.
For many people lifting weights has changed their life by opening up many new opportunities and offering countless benefits. The main benefit that we at ActiveGoals have seen is the amazing positive effects on mental health. All types of exercise release endorphins which is a feel-good chemical. The endorphins released from a regular exercise schedule can actually be enough to cure minor depression! This is why encourage everyone to carry out some sort of exercise on a fairly regular basis, what's the worst that could happen?!

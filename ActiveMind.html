Here at ActiveMind, we are all about bringing fitness enthusiasts together to share and discuss the impact fitness has had on their life, both physically and mentally. ActiveMind is creating communities in which people can share and discuss their lifestyle changes, journeys and other personal fitness related topics. Everyone here at ActiveMind is passionate about enlightening the public on how powerful exercise can be. We are also here to look past the gym stereotypes and see exercise for what it really is: the ultimate medication for the mind and body.

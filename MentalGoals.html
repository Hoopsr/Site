What is a 'Mental Goal'?

Mental Goals will be the section where we focus on mental health. These goals do not necessarily have to be directly linked with exercise as we have the other sub-salons to discuss them goals. Mental Goals is the last discussion topic as we feel it will be a good continuation of personal goals. We encourage discussion on mental health here as other, general goals will be discussed in the personal goals section. As mentioned earlier, feel free to talk about your experiences with mental health outside of fitness as we know it is such a large topic!
We know mental health can be a sensitive topic and therefore if you feel you're not ready to open up and tell the world, then we still encourage you to attend! Hearing other people in a similar situation and their experiences can go a long way in terms of knowing you're not going through this alone. One in four people across the world suffers from a mental health issue (over 1.8 billion) so please remember you're not alone! If you feel things are starting to go downhill then please get help - that's what it's there for! We hope to see you at the salon in April 2019!

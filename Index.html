As part of the ActiveMind series, ActiveGoals will be holding a salon on April 2019 discussing the important issue of mental health in our society. We will also be talking about exercise and how it goes hand-in-hand with medicating mental health issues. If you have ever had mental health issues and have used exercise to help medicate it then your presence might just help someone for the better.

Likewise, if you're in need of tips and advice for using exercise as medication for mental health, then this salon series is perfect for you! ActiveGoals will be focusing on a range of goal setting areas such as Mental Goals, Personal Goals, Strength Goals and Cardio Goals.
Make sure you mark April 2019 in your calendar!
